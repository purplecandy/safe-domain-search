Why use Safe Domain Search?

Most domain checkers aren't truly private. When you search for a domain on typical websites, your query may be logged or even shared with third parties. This opens you up to a shady practice called domain front-running.

Domain front-running is when someone — often a registrar — sees your search and registers the domain themselves, either to resell it to you at a premium or block you from getting it. This isn’t a myth — people have caught major registrars doing this.

Safe Domain Search avoids that. Your search stays on your machine. No analytics, no tracking, no risk of frontrunners getting a heads-up.
